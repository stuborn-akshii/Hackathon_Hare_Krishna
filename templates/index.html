About

Our Mission

At Gesture2Health, our mission is to revolutionize the healthcare experience for hearing-impaired individuals in India by eliminating communication barriers. We are committed to developing cutting-edge technology that accurately recognizes and translates Indian sign language (ISL) gestures into written text in real-time. By bridging the communication gap between hearing-impaired patients and healthcare professionals, we strive to provide inclusive and effective healthcare services that ensure every individual receives the care they deserve. Through our innovative solutions, we aim to create a more equitable and accessible healthcare system, empowering hearing-impaired patients to communicate their needs and receive optimal care with dignity and understanding.

Problem

The issue of communication barriers between hearing-impaired patients and healthcare professionals is a significant problem in India, where there are an estimated 18 million hearing-impaired individuals. These individuals often face significant challenges in accessing healthcare services due to a lack of communication resources and support. Many healthcare professionals are not trained in ISL or other sign languages, making it difficult for them to communicate effectively with hearing-impaired patients. This can lead to misunderstandings, misdiagnosis, and inadequate treatment, which can have serious consequences for the health and well-being of hearing-impaired individuals.

Solution

To address this problem, Gesture2Health has developed a state-of-the-art sign language recognition model trained on an Indian dataset for the healthcare domain. The model can recognise a wide range of ISL gestures and translate them into written text in real-time, enabling healthcare professionals to communicate effectively with hearing-impaired patients. The system uses a camera to capture the patient's gestures, which are then processed and analyzed by the model. The model then generates written text output, which can be used by healthcare professionals to communicate with the patient.

Our Initiative

Gesture2Health is a significant step towards providing more inclusive and effective healthcare services for hearing-impaired patients in India. The project has the potential to significantly improve the quality of healthcare for hearing-impaired individuals by removing communication barriers that can prevent them from receiving adequate care. The project also has important implications for the broader healthcare industry, highlighting the potential of technology to improve healthcare outcomes by addressing critical issues like communication barriers.

Gesture2Health is not just a technological solution, but also a social one. The project is designed to promote greater awareness and understanding of the needs of hearing-impaired patients in healthcare settings and to promote more inclusive and equitable healthcare services. By developing a sign language recognition model trained on an Indian dataset for the healthcare domain, Gesture2Health is helping to bridge the gap between hearing-impaired patients and healthcare professionals, and creating a more just and equitable healthcare system for all.
